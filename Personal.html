Beyond the Résumé

There's more to me than what you see on paper. My passions span a variety of creative and curious pursuits that inspire and challenge me in unique ways. These interests keep me grounded, motivated, and always eager to explore new perspectives — discover what drives me below.

Entertainment

Rotten Mango Podcast

When I'm not working on projects, you'll find me indulging in my eclectic hobbies. I'm a true crime podcast enthusiast, and one of my favorite podcasts is Rotten Mango. I love how host Stephanie Soo dives deep into crime cases, shedding light on victims' stories with empathy and detail. Her unbiased approach, storytelling skills, and the causes she supports make her podcast not only informative but impactful. It keeps me connected to what's happening in the world of true crime while also highlighting important social issues.

The Daily Ketchup Podcast

I love tuning into The Daily Ketchup for my dose of everyday news in Singapore, all served with a little extra sauce. This podcast keeps me updated on the latest headlines and stories, blending humor with insightful commentary. It's the perfect way for me to stay connected to what's happening in Singapore while enjoying a fresh perspective on the news.

Fitness

I understand the importance of staying fit, and I've found that archery is a great way to enhance my focus and build core strength. To support this, I enjoy practicing Pilates and yoga, which help improve my flexibility and balance. Together, these activities keep me physically active and centered.

Leisure

In my leisure time, I enjoy a variety of activities that bring me joy and relaxation. Reading allows me to explore new worlds and ideas, while crafting lets me express my creativity. I love hanging out with friends, making memories together, and capturing those moments through photography. Additionally, I find joy in doing my nails, as it's a fun way to unwind and add a touch of personal flair.

My Crochet Gallery
